# Diversity Resources

> NB: The following is forked from [ClojureBridge](https://github.com/ClojureBridge/organizing/blob/master/Resources-on-Diversity.md)
> We normally just link, but this list is exceptional

Below is a list of resources, created by people of diverse backgrounds, about how to facilitate inclusive spaces for (cis/trans) women, people of color, and people who identify as LGBTQIA.
